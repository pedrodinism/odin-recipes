Arroz de pato

Home



Description

A traditional baked rice dish made with shredded duck, chouriço, and aromatic flavors. It’s often served at family gatherings.

  • Duck
  • Chouriços
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Carrots
  • Long grain rice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Place the duck, 1 chouriço, carrot, bay leaves, salt, and pepper in a large pot. Cover with water and simmer until the duck is tender (about 1–1.5 hours).
  2. Remove the duck and chouriço; strain and reserve the cooking brot
  3. Shred the duck meat and slice the chouriço
  4. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add a splash of white wine.
  5. Stir in the rice, then add the reserved broth (about 2.5 parts broth for 1 part rice). Cook until the rice is almost done.
  6. LPreheat oven to 200°C (390°F).
  7. In a baking dish, layer half the rice, all the shredded duck, and then the rest of the rice.
  8. Turn off the heat
  9. Garnish the top with chouriço slices.
  10. Bake for about 20 minutes, until the top is golden and slightly crisp.
