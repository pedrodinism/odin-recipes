Description

Cod fish with french fries mixed with eggs

  • Potatoes
  • Cod fish
  • Onions
  • Coriander or Parsley
  • Eggs
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the potatoes
  2. Cut the onions in rings
  3. Add the onions to a pan, together with olive oil and a bay leaf
  4. Let it turn gold
  5. Add the cod fish and season with pepper
  6. Let it cook for 10 minutes
  7. Add the fries
  8. Turn off the heat
  9. Mix the eggs in a bowl
  10. Spill the eggs in the pan and mix it together
  11. Season with coriander or parsley
